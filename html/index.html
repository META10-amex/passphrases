Passphrases is a tool that helps you generate and memorize cryptographically secure passphrases.

Passphrases are generated using the Diceware method, and the memorization feature was partially inspired by the paper Towards reliable storage of 56-bit secrets in human memory, but Bonneau and Schechter.
